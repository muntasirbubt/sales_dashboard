SalesMatrix Dashboard

Empowering Data-Driven Sales Decisions

Overview

SalesMatrix Dashboard brings clarity and insights to your sales process with a modern, data-driven interface. Track performance, monitor KPIs, and visualize trends — all in one place.

  • ✔️ Date Range Filters – Analyze sales over any custom period.
  • ✔️ Key Metrics – Orders, revenue, top products, best salespersons, cancellations, and pending invoices.
  • ✔️ Interactive Charts – Bar, horizontal bar, pie, and doughnut visualizations.
  • ✔️ Responsive Design – Works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
  • ✔️ Real-Time Data – Accurate insights whenever filters are applied.

Sales Dashboard – KPIs & Filters

The first part of the dashboard provides an instant overview of your business performance. Use the date filters to analyze sales in any custom period. Quickly track top salespersons, payment status, sales order states, and best-selling products through dynamic widgets and charts.

Sales Dashboard – Insights & Analysis

The second part of the dashboard focuses on detailed analytics. Visualize sales trends, customer contributions, and team performance with interactive bar charts, pie charts, and donut charts. Get actionable insights into top customers, sales by value, and order statuses to make smarter business decisions.
